About

I’m Katarina, a floral designer and gardener based in the Berkshires in western Massachusetts. For the past few years, I’ve been gardening and creating arrangements for weddings & events throughout the New England area. I currently work at The Berkshire Botanical Garden in Stockbridge, MA. Previously, I grew cut flowers at Tiny Hearts in Copake, NY, and Allandale in Boston, MA.

For freelance and wedding inquiries, please get in touch with me at formosanafloral@gmail.com.
